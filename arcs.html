How to read the maturation column: the market and product arcs change verb each phase (widen→disconfirm→commit; cut→commit→iterate); the business arc keeps the verb and hardens the object (stress-test logic→numbers→sequence). Both are valid maturation, per adapt-posture.md.

Distinctness check: market acts on perception (what you can see / test), product acts on the artifact (what you build), business acts on defensibility (what survives a probe). No two rows share a move.
