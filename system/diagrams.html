A closed grammar of six primitives

Diagrams.

A diagram is a composed point, never an invention. Every chart, canvas, flow, matrix or model is assembled from one small grammar: field, node, connector, region, marker, annotation. A new diagram type is a new arrangement, not new ink. Colour is grouping — max three marks — never hierarchy; hierarchy is tone and position.

Transform pair

The brand's richer before/after — the right panel asserts as the green figure (the taught state), the left recedes (the naive state), and the hinge carries the single delta. Unit ledger & the kill line

Napkin economics: a column of +/− lines that resolve to a margin across a painted total rule, then the critical it dies if line. Red appears only here, as the brand's critical flag — the one condition under which a single unit does not survive. The unit formula sentence stays a formula line; this is the worked number behind it.
